The process

How a marriage intensive works

1

Free consultation

20-minute call to understand where you are, what you need, and whether an intensive is the right format. No pressure, no commitment.

2

Pre-session assessment

Both partners complete individual assessments before the intensive. This lets us hit the ground running instead of spending the first hours on intake.

3

The intensive

Concentrated, focused work in a private setting. We go deep into the patterns, the ruptures, and the underlying dynamics — with built-in breaks and structured exercises.

4

Follow-up sessions

Every intensive includes follow-up sessions to reinforce what you worked on, troubleshoot what came up, and make sure the changes stick.
